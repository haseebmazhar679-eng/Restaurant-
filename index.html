Our Story

A Dhaba Built on Good Food & Honest Prices

Kaka Hotel has been a familiar stop on the Bahawalnagar–Minchinabad Road for travellers, truck drivers, and local families alike. We cook the same way every day — fresh ingredients, simple recipes, and a kitchen that never closes.

Whether you're stopping in for a quick cup of chai or sitting down to a full plate of daal gosht, you'll always get something that's made with care and served with warmth.

  • Fresh food cooked every day, no shortcuts
  • Open 24 hours — yes, even at 3am
  • Affordable prices for everyone
  • Located right on the main Minchinabad Road
  • Seating available for families and groups
